For the love of god please follow naming and location conventions.

Things that will be changed and updated often go in the DYNAMIC folder. Blueprints, levels, and things that will frequently change.
Things that are more of a one-and-done go in the STATIC folder. Models, materials, textures, animations, etc.

Naming conventions are standard for the engine
MAT_* for materials
SEQ_* for sequences
NS_* for niagara systems

For imported assets try to orgainse based on their use in context
SND_Fireball_0
SND_Fireball_1
etc

If you are unsure just ask Nik. If have have assets bundled together that you'd prefer to keep in one place, put them in the best fitting folder.

Please don't dump things in the surface folder. Please remember to check out your branch.
Cheers
